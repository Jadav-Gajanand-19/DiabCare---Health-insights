Living Well with Diabetes

  • Master Your Plate: Learn about carb counting and the glycemic index. Prioritize non-starchy vegetables, lean proteins, and healthy fats.
  • Move Your Body: Combine aerobic exercise (like walking or swimming) with strength training to improve insulin sensitivity.
  • Monitor & Log: Check your blood sugar regularly as recommended by your doctor. Keep a log to understand how food and activity affect your levels.
  • Foot Care is Crucial: Check your feet daily for any cuts, sores, or changes in color. Always wear comfortable, well-fitting shoes.
  • Medication Adherence: Take your prescribed medications consistently and on time. Talk to your doctor before making any changes.
