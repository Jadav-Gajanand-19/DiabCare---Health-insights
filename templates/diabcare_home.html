Understanding Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body processes blood sugar (glucose). It's characterized by elevated levels of glucose in the blood, which can lead to serious health complications over time. There are several types of diabetes, including type 1, type 2, and gestational diabetes, each with its own causes and risk factors.
