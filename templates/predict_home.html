Welcome to Diabcare

Diabcare is a smart AI tool that helps predict whether a person may be at risk of diabetes using key medical indicators.

Why We Ask These Inputs

  • Pregnancies: Especially important in gestational diabetes.
  • Glucose: Direct indicator of blood sugar level.
  • Blood Pressure: High BP is a common comorbidity in diabetics.
  • Skin Thickness: Correlates with insulin resistance.
  • Insulin: Low or abnormal insulin levels suggest poor sugar regulation.
  • BMI: High BMI is a major diabetes risk factor.
  • Diabetes Pedigree Function (DPF): Measures genetic diabetes risk.
  • Age: Risk increases with age.
